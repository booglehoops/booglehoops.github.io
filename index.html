Magical Fluffy Pancakes

You can't code on an empty stomach,
these magical pancakes will keep you full until your next pizza party
To feed 4 humans or 1 hungry unicorn

Best Pancakes Evvarrr!
Sparkles McGee -Unicorn Team Leader

Ingredients

  • 200g of Unicorn Flour
  • 1 large Donkey Egg
  • 100g of Sparkle Sugar
  • 100g of Butter
  • 1 Metric Fuckton of Glitter
  • Cooking Oil

Method

  1. Cream together Butter and Sparkle Sugar
  2. Crack Egg into Mixture
  3. Add Unicorn Flour and stir until just mixed
  4. Heat pan and oil
  5. Pour one ladle of mixture into hot pan and heat for 2 minutes per side
  6. Garnish with plenty of glitter and enjoy!
